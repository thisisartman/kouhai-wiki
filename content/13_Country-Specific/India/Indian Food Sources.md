## Online Ordering *(Most Reliable for Specialty Items)*

Online ordering is often the most practical solution for hard-to-find ingredients.

**Reliable platforms:**

| Platform | What to Order | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Amazon Japan** | Spices, lentils, basmati rice, ghee, ready mixes | Fast delivery; Prime recommended |
| **iHerb** | Some Indian pantry items; good for health foods | Ships from US; 1–2 week delivery |
| **Kalustyan's JP** | Specialty spices | Verify if they ship to rural Niigata |
| **Cari.jp** | Indian grocery delivery in Japan | ⚠️ Verify current service availability |

**Amazon Japan search terms:**
- バスマティライス — basmati rice
- ギー — ghee
- チャパティ粉 / アタ粉 — atta flour for chapati

## Cooking Indian Food at IUJ

With spices sourced online + fresh vegetables from local supermarkets + rice from any grocery store, cooking Indian food at IUJ is very achievable.

**Easy dishes for dorm cooking:**
- Dal (any lentil variety + onion + tomato + spices)
- Aloo sabzi (potato + spices)
- Rajma (canned kidney beans work fine)
- Egg curry
- Simple biryani (basmati rice + spices + vegetables/eggs)
- Chai — bring tea masala from home or order online

> 💡 Bring a small supply of key spices from India when you first arrive — it's far easier than sourcing locally and the quality will be better. Essentials: garam masala, cumin seeds, mustard seeds, turmeric, coriander powder, red chili powder, hing (asafoetida).
