## Confirmed: Hours Change Seasonally

Per an Office of General Affairs email (June 29, 2026), the IUJ cafeteria's lunch/dinner hours are **not fixed year-round** — they get shortened during the summer break period. As one dated example (not a permanent schedule, don't rely on these exact times): in summer 2026, lunch shrank to a narrow 11:30–13:30 window with dinner closed most weekdays, briefly reopening dinner (12:00–19:00) on a Saturday, then settling into a reduced dinner slot (17:30–19:00) from around July 9 through the rest of the break. The attached School Shop kept its usual 8:00–19:00 hours throughout. Takeaway for readers: **expect reduced cafeteria hours during summer/winter breaks and confirm current hours via campus notices**, don't assume term-time hours apply. `unverified` for anything beyond this general pattern.
