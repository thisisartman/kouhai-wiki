IUJ teaches in English and most daily needs near campus can be managed without Japanese. But rural Niigata is not Tokyo — English is sparse outside campus. Even basic Japanese dramatically improves your quality of life here.

For IUJ's credit-bearing Japanese language courses, see [[Japanese Language Courses at IUJ]].

## Learning Apps & Resources

### For Building Foundation (Beginner)

| App / Resource | What It Does | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Duolingo** | Gamified daily practice; good for building habit | Free / Plus |
| **Hiragana/Katakana drills** | Any flashcard app; learn the scripts first | Free |
| **JapanesePod101** | Structured audio lessons; good for commuting | Free / Paid |
| **Anki** | Spaced-repetition flashcards; steep curve but powerful | Free |

### For Intermediate Progress

| App / Resource | What It Does | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Bunpro** | Grammar SRS — structured grammar study | Paid (reasonable) |
| **WaniKani** | Kanji learning via SRS; structured curriculum | Paid |
| **Satori Reader** | Graded reading practice; excellent quality | Paid |
| **Yomichan (browser extension)** | Hover-over dictionary for reading Japanese online | Free |

### For Everyday Use

| App / Resource | What It Does | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Translate (camera mode)** | Point camera at Japanese text → instant translation | Free |
| **DeepL** | Better translation quality than Google for nuanced text | Free / Pro |
| **Jisho.org** | Best Japanese–English dictionary | Free |
| **Google Maps (Japanese mode)** | Business names and directions in Japanese context | Free |

## Survival Phrases — Daily Life at IUJ

Learn these first. Pronunciation guide: vowels are pure (a=ah, i=ee, u=oo, e=eh, o=oh).

### Basics

| Japanese | Romaji | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| ありがとうございます | Arigatō gozaimasu | Thank you (formal) |
| すみません | Sumimasen | Excuse me / Sorry |
| はい / いいえ | Hai / Iie | Yes / No |
| わかりません | Wakarimasen | I don't understand |
| もう一度お願いします | Mō ichido onegaishimasu | Please say that again |
| 英語が話せますか？ | Eigo ga hanasemasu ka? | Do you speak English? |

### Shopping & Restaurants

| Japanese | Romaji | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| これをください | Kore o kudasai | I'll take this one |
| いくらですか？ | Ikura desu ka? | How much is it? |
| カードで払えますか？ | Kādo de haraemasu ka? | Can I pay by card? |
| PayPayで払えますか？ | PayPay de haraemasu ka? | Can I pay with PayPay? |
| 袋はいりません | Fukuro wa irimasen | I don't need a bag |
| ベジタリアンです | Bejitarian desu | I'm vegetarian |
| 肉なしでお願いします | Niku nashi de onegaishimasu | Without meat, please |
| アレルギーがあります | Arerugī ga arimasu | I have an allergy |

### Getting Around

| Japanese | Romaji | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| ～はどこですか？ | ~ wa doko desu ka? | Where is ~? |
| 駅はどこですか？ | Eki wa doko desu ka? | Where is the station? |
| 国際大学まで | Kokusai Daigaku made | To International University of Japan |
| 右 / 左 / まっすぐ | Migi / Hidari / Massugu | Right / Left / Straight ahead |
| 止まってください | Tomatte kudasai | Please stop (for taxis) |

### Emergencies

| Japanese | Romaji | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| 助けてください！ | Tasukete kudasai! | Help! |
| 救急車を呼んでください | Kyūkyūsha o yonde kudasai | Please call an ambulance |
| 警察を呼んでください | Keisatsu o yonde kudasai | Please call the police |
| 病院はどこですか？ | Byōin wa doko desu ka? | Where is the hospital? |
| 痛いです | Itai desu | It hurts |
