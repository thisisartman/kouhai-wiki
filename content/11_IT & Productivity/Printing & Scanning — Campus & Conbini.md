# 🖨️ Printing & Scanning — Campus & Conbini

You'll need to print more than you expect — assignment submissions, admin forms, visa documents, academic papers, official letters. Two options cover everything: the campus printers and convenience store (conbini) multifunction machines.

---

## Campus Printing

IUJ's library and computer labs have printers available for students. These are the primary option for academic printing.

> ⚠️ Confirm current pricing, print quota, and how to load credits with IUJ IT or the library desk — systems and costs vary year to year.

**Typical setup:**
- Log in with your IUJ student credentials
- Select a printer, send the document from your laptop or a campus computer
- Collect and pay (print credits deducted from your student account or paid per job)

**What campus printing is good for:**
- Lecture notes and readings
- Assignment printouts
- Academic papers and research articles

## Convenience Store Printing (Conbini)

Japan's convenience stores have multifunction machines (コピー機 / マルチコピー機) that print, scan, copy, and fax. They're excellent for everything the campus printer doesn't cover — especially documents you need printed quickly outside campus hours.

### Main Chains and Their Systems

| Chain | Service | App/Web |
|---|---|---|
| **7-Eleven** | netprint | [netprint.ne.jp](https://www.netprint.ne.jp) |
| **Lawson / FamilyMart** | PrintSmash / network print | App or [printsmash.jp](https://www.printsmash.jp) |

All systems work roughly the same way: upload your file online or via app, get a print code, enter it at the machine, pay and collect.

### Printing Costs

| Type | Cost |
|---|---|
| A4 Black & White | ¥10–20/sheet |
| A4 Colour | ¥50–60/sheet |
| A3 Black & White | ¥20–30/sheet |
| Photo print | ¥30–40 (L-size) |

### What You Can Print

- PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint files
- Photos (L size, 2L size)
- PDFs from URLs (some services)

### How to Use (Netprint Example)

1. Go to [netprint.ne.jp](https://www.netprint.ne.jp) or download the app
2. Upload your file (no account required for guests; account saves history)
3. Get an 8-digit print code
4. At any 7-Eleven machine: select "Print" → "Netprint" → enter code
5. Choose colour/BW, number of copies, pay cash

> 💡 Codes expire in 1 day (guest) or 30 days (registered). Save your code until you've confirmed the print.

## Scanning

Conbini multifunction machines scan to USB or email. Useful for:
- Scanning official documents (passport, residence card, certificates)
- Sending signed forms to IUJ admin or scholarship providers
- Digitising receipts for expense claims

**Cost:** ¥10–30 per scan depending on the machine and chain.

**How to scan at 7-Eleven:**
1. Place document face-down on the glass
2. Select "Scan" on the machine touchscreen
3. Choose output format (PDF, JPEG) and destination (USB drive or email)
4. Insert USB or enter your email address
5. Confirm and collect

## Fax (if Needed)

Japanese bureaucracy still uses fax occasionally. Conbini machines fax to domestic and international numbers. Rarely needed but good to know: it's available.
