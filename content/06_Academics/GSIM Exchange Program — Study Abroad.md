---

## The Process

1. **Application** — submitted through the relevant office during an announced window
2. **Interview** — a selection interview as part of the application process
3. **School assignment** — you're matched to a partner school, not free choice of any partner
4. **GPA requirement** — you need to **maintain a GPA of 3.30 or higher** to keep your nomination; falling below this can cost you the placement
5. **Acceptance** — formal acceptance from the partner school
6. **Visa** — arranging whatever visa/entry permission the host country requires
7. **Mandatory info session** — attendance required before departure
8. **Withdrawal process** — a defined process exists if you need to withdraw after being accepted; ask OAA for specifics if this applies to you

## Housing While on Exchange

You can generally **keep your IUJ dorm room** while on exchange, but you must **report absences of 60+ days** — this isn't automatic, so follow up with Dorm Staff/OSS about the reporting requirement before you leave.

## Lower-Commitment Alternative: Virtual MBA Exchange with WHU

A lighter-weight option exists alongside the full exchange: a **Virtual MBA Exchange with WHU** (online, limited slots). Worth considering if a full physical exchange isn't feasible for you.
