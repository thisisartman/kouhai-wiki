Setting up your dorm room in your first week — what's already there, what you'll want to buy, and where. Room dimensions and basic facilities are official; move-in day specifics (exact furniture, what's worth bringing) still need firsthand input — see Senior Submissions below.

---

## Room Dimensions

Per IUJ's own Dormitory Info:
- **SD1**: 14.3m², with a slightly larger unit bath than SD2/SD3. Ceiling height 2m50cm.
- **SD2 and SD3**: 15m², with a slightly smaller unit bath than SD1. Ceiling height 2m75cm.
- **MSA**: 2 unfurnished rooms (bedroom + kitchen/dining/living area) plus a small unit bath.

All single rooms (SD1-3) come with basic living facilities, a private bathroom, LAN + WiFi (personal routers recommended for stability), a peephole, and a chain lock. MSA is unfurnished — bedding and table rentals can be arranged separately.

## Getting Started

- Once you know what's already in your room, you can plan what to buy locally — see [[100 Yen & Recycle Shops — Budget Furnishing]] for cheap furnishing options nearby.
- SD4 and MSA rooms may differ from SD1–3 — see [[Dorm Life & Facilities]] for what's known about each building.
