IUJ's on-campus gym is free to use (see [[Student Discounts]], [[Mental Health Resources]]) and spans two floors.

---

## Gym (2nd Floor)

- **Women's Only Hour**: reserved strictly for women every **Tuesday, Wednesday, and Sunday, 6:00–8:00 PM**. Please respect this.
- Check the official reservation schedule for other time slots.
- Children are **not allowed** in this gym, for safety and hygiene reasons.
- Also houses the **Music Room** and the fitness area — per FT-OGA (Facilities), these normally stay open even when the 1st-floor arena is closed for maintenance.

## Gym (1st Floor)

- Remove outdoor shoes before entering; use the temporary shoe lockers.
- Wear clean indoor sports shoes or slippers inside.
- Take your shoes with you when you leave. Long-term storage in the lockers isn't permitted. Per FT-OGA reminders, shoes and personal items left in the shoe racks or shower room around high-traffic events (e.g. the International Festival) get removed and confiscated if not collected in time — don't leave things behind "just for a few days."

## Hours

- The gymnasium is **closed from midnight to 6:00 AM**, per FT-OGA (Facilities) — relevant if you're relying on it as a hot-shower backup during a dorm hot-water outage (see [[Dorm Life & Facilities]]).

## Drinking Water

- The water dispensers inside the gym are **strictly for drinking**, not for washing hands, faces, or personal items. Use the restroom sinks for that instead.

---

## Bookings & Closures

Like other campus spaces (classrooms, [[Dorm Life & Facilities|CNP]], and MLIC Hall), the gym can be **booked** for events and can be **closed** for the duration of specific campus events during the year. Check with OSS/GSO if you have a specific date in mind rather than assuming it's always open.

Beyond one-off event bookings, the **1st-floor arena periodically closes entirely for maintenance** — confirmed examples per FT-OGA (Facilities) include an annual floor-waxing closure (arena + storage-room access both unavailable for several days) and, separately, ad-hoc repair work when equipment defects turn up (e.g. a fire-damper repair requiring loud concrete-chipping work, announced with advance notice and a call for students to flag dates they need the arena kept free). The 2nd floor (Music Room, fitness area) typically stays open during 1st-floor arena closures. If you have a storage locker or a fixed event date, check with FT-OGA rather than assuming access.
